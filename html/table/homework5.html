Cambodia Flowers

1. Rumdul

The national flower of Cambodia. It has 
a sweet fragrance and is often used in traditional ceremonies.
                    
2. Lotus

A sacred flower in Cambodian culture. It symbolizes 
purity and enlightenment.
                    
3. Frangipani

A beautiful flower with a strong fragrance. 
It’s often used in traditional Cambodian decorations.
                    
4. Hibiscus

A vibrant flower that represents beauty and passion 
in Cambodian culture.
